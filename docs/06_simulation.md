# Simulation

## Objective

The objective of this phase is to test the inventory policy under uncertainty using Monte Carlo simulation.

## Scenarios Simulated

- Demand variability
- Lead time variability
- Stockout risk
- Cost impact

## Inputs

- Average demand
- Demand standard deviation
- Safety stock
- Reorder point
- Lead time mean
- Lead time standard deviation

## Outputs

- Fill rate
- Stockout probability
- Average simulated cost

## Business Usage

Simulation helps validate whether the calculated safety stock and reorder points are strong enough under real-world uncertainty.

This phase supports:

- Stockout prevention
- Inventory policy validation
- Risk-based inventory planning
- Service-level testing
